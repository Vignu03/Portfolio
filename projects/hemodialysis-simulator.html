HemoDialysis Simulator

An immersive XR simulation that replicates the hemodialysis procedure in a fully interactive 3D environment — designed for medical education, clinical training, and patient awareness.

C# Unity Engine Unity XR Medical Simulation

Developed a comprehensive XR simulation of the hemodialysis procedure — a life-sustaining treatment used when the kidneys can no longer filter waste and excess fluid from the blood. The simulation allows users to walk through every step of the dialysis process in an immersive VR environment, providing a safe, repeatable learning experience for medical students and nursing staff.

The simulator accurately models blood circuit setup, dialyzer function, fluid removal, and alarm responses. Using Unity XR Toolkit, users interact with real dialysis machine components via VR controllers — building procedural muscle memory without risk to real patients.

What is Hemodialysis?

Hemodialysis is a medical procedure performed when the kidneys fail to adequately remove waste products (urea, creatinine), excess water, and harmful toxins from the blood. A dialysis machine acts as an artificial kidney — blood is drawn from the patient's body, passed through a dialyzer (semi-permeable membrane filter), and returned cleaned. Sessions typically last 3–5 hours and occur 3 times per week.

  • Blood is accessed via an arteriovenous (AV) fistula, graft, or central venous catheter
  • A blood pump drives blood through the extracorporeal (outside the body) circuit
  • The dialyzer contains thousands of hollow semi-permeable fibres — waste diffuses out across the membrane into the dialysate solution
  • Dialysate flows counter-current to blood to maximise diffusion gradient efficiency
  • Ultrafiltration removes excess fluid by applying controlled transmembrane pressure
  • Anticoagulants (e.g. heparin) prevent blood clotting in the circuit
  • Air detectors and pressure monitors ensure patient safety throughout the session

How the Simulation Works

The XR simulator guides users through a structured, step-by-step hemodialysis session. Each phase is interactive — users must correctly perform actions in sequence, mimicking real clinical protocols. Incorrect steps trigger educational feedback, not failure states, reinforcing learning rather than penalising mistakes.

  • Machine Setup: Prime the blood tubing set, connect dialyzer, and configure dialysate flow rate
  • Patient Connection: Needle insertion (AV fistula simulation), blood line connection, and heparin bolus administration
  • Session Monitoring: Real-time blood pressure, venous/arterial pressure alarms, and fluid balance tracking
  • Troubleshooting Mode: Respond to common alarms — access infiltration, air-in-line detection, low blood pressure events
  • Disconnection Protocol: Safely return blood to patient, remove needles, apply haemostasis

Key Features

  • Step-by-step guided hemodialysis procedure with interactive VR component handling
  • Accurate 3D models of dialysis machine, dialyzer, blood tubing sets, and access sites
  • Real-time blood flow simulation with dynamic pressure and flow-rate visualisation
  • Alarm simulation module for hands-on clinical troubleshooting practice
  • Patient safety feedback — warnings for incorrect sequencing or unsafe actions
  • XR Grab Interactables for manipulating machine knobs, clamps, and tubing connectors
  • Designed for medical colleges, nursing schools, and nephrology training centres
