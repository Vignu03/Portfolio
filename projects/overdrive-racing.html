Overdrive: Multi-Mode Racing

A feature-rich 3D car racing game built in Unity featuring 5 distinct game modes across unique environments, 5 unlockable high-performance cars, and a star-based progression system that keeps every race rewarding.

C# Unity Engine Game Development 3D Racing

Overdrive is a high-octane 3D car racing game developed in Unity, designed to deliver a varied and exciting racing experience across five completely different game modes and environments. Each mode challenges players with unique objectives — from classic lap racing to lunar low-gravity drifting — ensuring no two races feel the same.

Players earn stars by completing races and achieving objectives within each mode. Stars unlock new game modes, tracks, and an elite garage of five meticulously modelled real-world-inspired cars — rewarding skill progression throughout the game.

Game Modes

  • 🏁 Classical Mode — The purest form of racing. Compete head-to-head on traditional circuits, complete laps in the shortest time, and fight for pole position. Perfect for mastering car handling and racing lines.
  • ❄️ Escape Mode — Snow Area — A high-tension chase through icy, snow-covered terrain. Players must outrun pursuing opponents on slippery roads where traction is everything. Dynamic weather effects and reduced grip make every turn a calculated risk.
  • 🏜️ Survival Mode — Desert Area — Survive as long as possible against relentlessly spawning obstacles and rival cars across scorching desert highways. Increasing difficulty ramps up speed and hazard density — last car running wins.
  • 🌆 Infection Mode — Urban City — A frantic urban chase mode set in a dense city environment. One car starts "infected" and must tag others; infected cars spread the infection. Survive the longest without being tagged to claim victory.
  • 🌕 Special Mode — Moon — Race on the lunar surface under low-gravity physics. Cars float slightly over jumps, drifts last longer, and collision trajectories are completely unpredictable — a truly out-of-this-world racing experience.

Unlockable Cars

Each car has unique handling characteristics — different top speeds, acceleration curves, cornering grip, and drift behaviour — encouraging players to choose the right machine for each mode and environment.

  • 🚗 Dodge Challenger — A muscle car powerhouse. High top speed and raw straight-line acceleration, but demanding to control through tight corners. Ideal for Classical and Desert modes.
  • 🚙 Mitsubishi Lancer — The rally legend. Exceptional all-terrain grip and precise steering make it the go-to choice for the Snow Escape mode on slippery surfaces.
  • 🚐 Range Rover Evoque — A versatile SUV with superior stability. Handles rough terrain and obstacles confidently — well suited for Survival and Infection modes where durability matters.
  • 🏎️ Peugeot Onyx — A sleek concept supercar with a perfect balance of speed and agility. Excels on smooth urban circuits in Infection Mode and delivers a polished driving feel throughout.
  • 🏎️ Lamborghini Huracán — The crown jewel of the garage. Blistering top speed, aggressive aerodynamics, and stunning visuals. Unlocked only by the highest star earners — worth every race it takes.

Star-Based Progression System

Every race awards up to 3 stars based on performance criteria — finishing position, completion time, and mode-specific objectives (e.g. survival duration, infection avoidance). Stars accumulate across all modes and are spent to unlock content.

  • ⭐ 1 Star — Race completion (reach the finish / survive the minimum threshold)
  • ⭐⭐ 2 Stars — Strong performance (podium finish or above-average survival time)
  • ⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars — Mastery (1st place win or maximum survival/infection avoidance)
  • Stars unlock new game modes — later modes require cumulative stars to access
  • Car unlocks are tiered by total stars — rarer cars need more accumulated stars
  • Encourages replayability — revisiting earlier modes to earn missed stars

Key Features

  • 5 distinct game modes across 5 unique themed environments
  • 5 unlockable cars with individual handling profiles and physics tuning
  • Star-based progression system gating modes and car unlocks
  • Environment-specific physics — ice traction loss, desert heat haze, low lunar gravity
  • Dynamic AI opponents that adapt difficulty per mode and star level
  • Polished 3D environments with themed lighting and atmosphere per mode
  • Smooth arcade-style car physics with realistic drift and collision response
  • Two players can play at a time with customised settings
  • In-game garage with car selection and stats preview before each race
